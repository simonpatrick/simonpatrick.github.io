I'm really addicted for beautiful offices, actually I know that most of you guys are too. One thing that most of the tech companies are thinking on the project is brainstorm rooms, spaces projected for creativity and communication.

But what these rooms must have to be so special and dedicated to that puporse? They should allow draw and write on the walls, be colourful and to be perfect, got some different chairs and things to sit. Simple, isn't it?
